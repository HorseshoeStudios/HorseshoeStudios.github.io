History

The main character is a broken man. Then we have a fight with our wife for some reason, and we break up. While watching TV at home with our child Lily, we see a circus commercial and Lily insists that we go to the circus and we go to the circus and buy 2 tickets. A security guard waits at the door to enter the circus and after we show our tickets, suddenly the lights go out and we faint. After waking up, we set out to look for our child. But we don't know anything about the circus, so we get information from former circus workers. After we familiarize with the circus, we go on a search and we encounter horrible creatures. We try to kill these creatures with mind-boggling puzzles and finally we find an escape route and try to escape with Lily, but Lily doesn't want to come... Because the circus gets everything it wants to get.
